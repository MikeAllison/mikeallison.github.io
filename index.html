About Me

I’ve been building software engineering chops over the last few years by taking online courses and building personal projects while holding a full-time job in IT support. I’m hoping that there’s a company out there that will take the chance to allow me to contribute more to this world than just telling people to turn their computers off and back on again. I love solving problems with code and learning new skills.

I’ve created this site to showcase some of my better projects and skills. I’ve taken a variety of courses from sites such as Treehouse, Code School, Pluralsight, Launch School, Udemy, and Udacity. These topics include: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Ruby on Rails, SASS, Bootstrap, Foundation, and responsive design.

Recently, I’ve learned about using development tools such as Gulp, Grunt, npm, and Bower. I’m currently working on a personal project utilizing a Backbone front end with a Rails 5 API back end.

In addition to programming, I enjoy playing guitar, writing music, cooking, and brewing beer.
